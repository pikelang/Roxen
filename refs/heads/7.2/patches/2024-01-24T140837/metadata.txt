• FTP: Do not send stderr output from the internal ls (ie LIST or NLST).

Some ftp clients assume that LIST and NLST will return an empty
output if the argument does not exist.

Fixes [REPD-937].

• ConfigIF: Do not save variables for faked modules.

Saving the variables for faked modules may zap existing settings
for modules that eg have a compilation error.

Fixes [EP-2051].

• Variable.Schedule: More fixing of DST transitions.

Fixes transition out of DST for times during the first hour
the day after the transition.

Fixes [REPD-922].
